# Privacy Policy for Social Reposter

This Privacy Policy describes how the Social Reposter application handles data.

## Data Collection and Use
*   **Personal Data**: This application does not collect or store any personal identifying information.
*   **Platform Data**: The application accesses your YouTube and TikTok accounts via official APIs solely to automate the cross-posting of your own content.
*   **Storage**: All credentials (API tokens) and state files are stored locally on your own machine in the application directory. No data is sent to any third-party servers other than the official Google (YouTube) and TikTok APIs.

## Third-Party Services
This application interacts with:
*   Google APIs (YouTube Data API)
*   TikTok API
*   Meta API (Instagram Graph API)

Please refer to their respective privacy policies for how they handle your data.
